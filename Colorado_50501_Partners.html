Solidarity Warrioriors

Solidarity Warriors is an action-focused, national group of people who are dedicated to making real change in the U.S. We are a chapter of the Indivisible movement. We put pressure on legislators through mass calls to their offices and town halls, donate to legal defense funds, support striking workers, protect vulnerable communities such as LGBTQ+ and immigrants, and serve the community.

Move On

MoveOn members are a force for social justice and political progress. We come from all 50 states and all walks of life. Together, we are at once large and nimble, targeting our resources purposefully, yet pivoting quickly as we identify new opportunities for change and mobilize to seize them.
